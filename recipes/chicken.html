Air Fryer Chicken

Description

Tender and Juicy Air Fryer Chicken is golden crispy outside and moist and delicate on the inside. This is bursting with flavor and so good!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts: Buying skinless, boneless make it super easy.
  • Avocado Oil: Perfect mild flavored oil for the air fryer
  • Lime Juuice: This really adds a certain freshness to the chicken
  • Chile Lime Seasoning: You can find this at Trader Joe's
  • Optional: Brussel sprouts

Steps

  1. Oil: Place the chicken in the fryer and rub with the avocado oil to coat
  2. Combine: In a small bowl combine the lime juice and seasoning and rub generously on the chicken
  3. Air Fry: Bake at 360 for 9 min. Flip (add brussel sprouts if using) Cook for another 9 min or till internal temp is 165.
